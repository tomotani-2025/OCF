BATWA FARM AT MATANDA HARVEST JULY AUGUST  2024

Jul 27
Written By LES OMOTANI

 

The Batwa Farm at Matanda continues to be very productive and a source of pride, nutrition and satisfaction.  Most of all the farm provides hope for the future!  In August the Batwa will participate in the harvest of the next crop of corn maize!  The new farm house is a great addition to the farm and we are grateful to those who supported its construction.  After the harvest the farm house will be completed and painted.

LES OMOTANI
