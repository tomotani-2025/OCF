## BASE CAMP FOR VETERANS REUNION

## Batwa Farm yields 1.67 tonnes of maize flour

The Omotani Caring Foundation has donated funds to support the purchase of a new Singer sewing machine, a sweater sewing machine, fabric, thread and other materials to support the project.   These first, next steps, will allow the women to develop increased independence for themselves and their families.

NEW SWEATER MACHINE

o This program was started in 2020 by Robinah Gangiriba, and has currently more than 50 members. They are all women of underprivileged backgrounds o They make handicrafts (baskets, clothing, and other items) and also perform music/dance/drama. o Click here to learn more.
