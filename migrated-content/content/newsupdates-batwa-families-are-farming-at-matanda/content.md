# Batwa families are farming at Matanda!

## BWINDI PLUS GIRLS PROJECT

## Sir Edmund Hillary Memorial Museum and Elder Care Center

Batwa farm at Matanda

Batwa families are working at the farm at Matanda.

Every year the family members learn how to become active and effective farmers.

They are learning how to grow their own food and provide for the future food needs of their families.  Food independence is a very desirable and achievable goal for these Batwa families.

This farm project continues to be a great success and model for creating a sustainable future.
