## Farmhouse at Batwa Farm at Matanda

## Women in Gorilla Conservation - next steps!

BASECAMP REUNION MAY 5 2024

Each month veterans and their families return to the White Stallion Ranch for a “reunion.”  Each session reinforces the progress and successful transition steps that began with their first week long Base Camp experience.  Next Base Camp week long experience is scheduled for May 19, 2024

https://www.facebook.com/BaseCampforVeteransInc/

BaseCamp for Veterans Inc. is at White Stallion Ranch.

12h  · Picture Rocks, AZ  ·

This last Sunday, May 5, 2024,  marks our last of the monthly BCI clinics before Class 8!! This clinic was the first to include some enrichment for our petting zoo animals! Class 7 your participation each month and seamless transition into the larger BCI community has been magical to watch! BCI Class 8 we are excited to meet you
