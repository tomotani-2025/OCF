# Batwa Farm has more progress!

## TPN COMMUNITY POND

## 2024-25 Report and update from The Partners Nepal

The Batwa Farm at Matanda continues to receive support and makes steady progress.  Some of the Batwa families are spending more and more days at the farm to do ongoing work.  A new latrine was built on site at the farm.
