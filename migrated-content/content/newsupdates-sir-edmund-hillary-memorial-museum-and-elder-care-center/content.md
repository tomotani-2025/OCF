# Sir Edmund Hillary Memorial Museum and Elder Care Center

## Batwa families are farming at Matanda!

## TPN - One Day One Tree in Chheskam

The Partners Nepal:

Sir Edmund Hillary Memorial Museum and Elder Care Center

The Omotani Caring Foundation continues to provide support for the Hillary Elder Care Center at Bung, Nepal.

A new washing machine and dryer has been purchased for the elderly home and is being shipped to Bung.  In addition, a variety of kitchen cooking and serving supplies have also been provided as requested by the local people.  The OCF is also providing new flooring carpet and clothes as requested.  Shipping and transportation costs have also been donated by the OCF.

The OCF has made additional donations to TPN and the Elder Care Center in honor and memory of Valerie Sauer, Alberta, Canada.
