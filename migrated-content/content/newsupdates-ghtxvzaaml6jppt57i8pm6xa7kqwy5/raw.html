BASE CAMP FOR VETERANS and RANDY HELM HORSEMANSHIP 

Mar 26
Written By LES OMOTANI

 Each year BaseCamp for Veterans is actively providing veterans with two week long clinics at the White Stallion Ranch. In addition monthly follow up sessions are facilitated for participants from previous classes.

https://www.bcampinc.com

 https://www.facebook.com/BaseCampforVeteransInc/

BaseCamp for Veterans Inc.

March 10 at 5:15 PM  · 

 

BCI’s first clinic with Class 7 in attendance! Mix of round pen work, a scavenger hunt in the herd, and some brushing (shedding those winter coats!), mane and tail care and even some hoof conditioner. Most importantly the BCI community spent time together. Thank you all for coming out!

 

 https://www.facebook.com/BaseCampforVeteransInc

 

The Omotani Caring Foundation is pleased to support the ongoing participation of the Mustang Horsemanship Program Facilitator – Randy Helm.  We also support the participation of a veteran in a future week-long Base Camp Program.  The next Base Camp week long program is being held at White Stallion Ranch in May, 2024.
