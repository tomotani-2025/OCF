Batwa Farm at Matanda update May 4 2025

May 4
Written By LES OMOTANI


Mushamba Moses and several members of the Batwa families are working on the farm at Matanda.  We are going to focus our efforts at continuing to make this farm an ongoing sustainable source of food for the Batwa families.  We continue to be happy that the farm is able to produce sources of food and often allows for two harvests each year.

 

LES OMOTANI
