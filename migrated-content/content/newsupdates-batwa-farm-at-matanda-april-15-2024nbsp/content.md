# BATWA FARM AT MATANDA APRIL 15 2024

## Batwa Farm yields 1.67 tonnes of maize flour

## THE PARTNERS NEPAL

TWO STEPS FORWARD….

During the second week of April approximately 2231 KG of corn was taken for grinding into maize flour.  We will soon deliver the many bags of maize flour to the Batwa families.

The Batwa farm at Matanda is successfully feeding many families!
