# Goal Three:  Health

#### FOOD AND MEDICINE = $5,000Develop an emergency reserve fund of $5,000 to be used for future purchases and distribution of foods rich in protein such as maize / corn flour, beans and fruits. Also provide for the purchase of soap, masks, medicine and other emergency supplies. During the 2020 pandemic, individual amounts ranging from $1,000 - $1,500 and totaling over $10,000 USD supported the purchase and distribution of food, soap and medical supplies to students and their families, teachers, Batwa families, and porters in nearby villages.
