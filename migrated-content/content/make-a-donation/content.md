# Donations…

Dear interested donor and supporter,

The Omotani Caring Foundation is a private family foundation.  We do accept donations from family and close friends who want to support approved projects under our Goal Seven category or specific project goals that are meaningful to the individual.

We maintain this OCF website as the only digital media that describes the work of our foundation.  It is NOT intended to solicit donations from others.  We are happy to personally fund the good work of our foundation’s projects!  There are many ways for you to directly support the groups and projects that are supported by the OCF.

We provide over 95% of the funding for the Omotani Caring Foundation from our personal donations and funds.   We also pay for all operational, legal and accounting fees and costs for our foundation.

Please understand that is NOT the purpose of our maintaining this OCF website.  We maintain this website to allow people to easily understand the projects supported by the OCF.

The OCF is a 501 c 3 charity recognized by the federal department of revenue.  If you remain interested in possibly supporting one or more of the goals and projects of our foundation you are welcome to donate.

After you click the DONATE button you will be able to choose and specify which of the goals and projects you wish to support.

This option will appear under the ADDITIONAL INFORMATION section.

If you prefer to donate using a bank account check please specify which of the project goals you wish to support and mail it to the following address:OMOTANI CARING FOUNDATION8337 N Lee Trevino DriveTucson, Arizona, USA85742

If you wish to receive a receipt for your donation please include your email and mailing address.

PayPal provides you and our foundation with a secure method of processing your donation. As a registered and IRS approved USA 501 (c)(3) charity each donation to the OMOTANI CARING FOUNDATION is transacted by PayPal for a reduced fee of 2.2% + thirty cents. If you wish to avoid this transaction fee please mail a check for your donation to the foundation.

The OMOTANI CARING FOUNDATION transfers 100% of all funds received from donors for our designated projects. All administrative, accounting and legal fees are paid for by the trustees.

Pay Pal offers discounted transaction rates for confirmed 501 c 3 charities for most products.
