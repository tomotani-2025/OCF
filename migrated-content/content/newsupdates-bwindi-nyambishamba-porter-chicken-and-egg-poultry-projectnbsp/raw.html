BWINDI NYAMBISHAMBA PORTER CHICKEN AND EGG POULTRY PROJECT 

Jun 17
Written By LES OMOTANI

Milton Byamukama  and Paul Kirui have provided photos of the first chicks to arrive for the project.   This is the first step in making this Bwindi Nyambishamba Porter Chicken and Egg project a reality.  We hope that in 4 -5 months the chickens will begin to lay eggs.
