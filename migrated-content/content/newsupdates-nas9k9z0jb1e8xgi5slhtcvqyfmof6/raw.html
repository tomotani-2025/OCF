RANDY HELM HORSEMANSHIP

Mar 26
Written By LES OMOTANI

Randy Helm Horsemanship is focused on horse training in clinics, demonstrations, and expos. The clinics are usually 2 to 3-day events with horses and riders.

Whether clinics or expos, we focus on becoming a safe, confident rider with a responsive, safe horse.

Randy has conducted clinics and expos for numerous organizations such as Arizona State Prison, US Border Patrol, Law Enforcement, and of course the general public.

 

Randy’s support and leadership with BaseCamp for Veterans and his ongoing support for the graduates of the Arizona Prison Inmate Horsemanship program combine his passion for helping wild mustangs and deserving people to find a great future through the learning of horsemanship! 

 

The Omotani Caring Foundation is grateful for the opportunity to support and also benefit from Randy Helm Horsemanship.
