BATWA FARM AT MATANDA JUNE 2024

Progress continues on the new farmhouse for the Batwa Farm at Matanda.    Additional corn/maize flour was distributed to the Batwa families last week.  We continue to be very happy about the many steps forward for this farming project that is providing self-sufficiency and a plentiful food supply for the Batwa families.
