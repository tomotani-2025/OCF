# Batwa Farm yields 1.67 tonnes of maize flour

## Women in Gorilla Conservation - next steps!

## BATWA FARM AT MATANDA APRIL 15 2024

The Batwa Farm at Matanda produced a very  large amount of maize flour [posho] for 2024

The second harvest at the Batwa Farm at Matanda has produced approximately 1.67 tonnes of maize flour.  The flour has been stored in individual bags and distributed to the Batwa families.

The Batwa farm project is proving to be a successful experience in creating a sustainable food supply for the future.
