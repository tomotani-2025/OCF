Our Mission

Supporting communities through sustainable initiatives around the world.

What We Do

We provide basic food and other supplies for many families (including the Batwa) and porters in rural villages.

We support the self-identified needs of schools that serve local and native children in the USA and throughout the world. We are advocates for children with special needs and talents.

We support projects that promote the ability of local people to meet the challenges of food scarcity and to preserve their indigenous cultures.

We support the dreams and visions of charitable leaders who seek to provide care and support to children and families in local communities throughout the world.

We support servant leaders who provide caring services, education and mentoring for military veterans and their families, first responders, prison inmates, native peoples, equine trainers and ranchers, and others as they learn modern horsemanship to gentle and train wild mustangs. We also support those who help provide forever homes for the mustangs.

We support the conservation efforts of local, national and international individuals and groups who work to ensure the future health of natural environments, marine life and wildlife.

Our Mission Goals

1

Sustainability: Batwa Farms

Supporting the Batwa families through the purchase of 10 acres of fertile farmland at Matanda, Uganda. Over $23,000 invested in 2022 with ongoing support for farming operations.

Learn More
2

Porters at Bwindi

Supporting food-related sustainability projects for porter associations, including grants for bees and chickens. 35 bee hives already producing honey.

Learn More
3

Health

Emergency reserve fund for distribution of protein-rich foods, soap, masks, medicine and other emergency supplies to communities in need.

4

Technology

Supporting technology access including computers, printers, and digital cameras for school children. Over $3,500 provided for technology in Bwindi schools.

5

Fresh Water

Supporting fresh water access through wells, water treatment systems, and alternative strategies for underserved Native American families and villages worldwide.

6

Education

K-12 and higher education support, including tuition fees, special education services, and programs like TROT, Special Olympics, and Unified Sports.

7

Donor Requests

Supporting meaningful charitable initiatives proposed by donors, including the William J Semon Family Resource Fund and SIBS PLACE program.

8

The Partners Nepal

Strengthening relationships with The Partners Nepal (TPN), providing US citizens an easy way to donate and support TPN's conservation projects.

9

Women in Gorilla Conservation

Collaborating with Sahaya International and Rafiki Wildlife to support women's conservation initiatives in Uganda.

Learn More
10

Base Camp for Veterans

Supporting BCI's mission to help veterans through personal and professional growth programs, including mustang training with Randy Helm.

Learn More
11

Randy Helm Horsemanship

Supporting the training of wild mustangs and helping them find forever homes. Working with trainers dedicated to the positive transformational approach.

High Asia Habitat Fund

Supporting snow leopard conservation and helping local communities coexist with wildlife in the high Himalayan mountains.
