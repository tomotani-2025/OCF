Make a Donation

Your contribution makes a difference in communities around the world.

100% of Your Donation Goes to Projects

All funds donated by family, friends and others (100%) are transferred and used to complete the designated project(s). The trustees independently and personally pay for all administrative, accounting and legal fees.

Important Information

The Omotani Caring Foundation does NOT actively solicit donations. We personally fund over 95% of the OCF projects and activities.

When directly asked by individuals, we provide caring citizens who reside in the United States of America with an easy, legal and trustworthy process to donate and support deserving children and families.

SIBS Place Program

The Omotani Caring Foundation has provided ongoing support for the SIBS PLACE program since 2000. SIBSPLACE (Survivorship in Brothers and Sisters) is a free program serving well children ages 5-17, who are living with a sibling with cancer or another devastating medical illness, or a parent with cancer.

Learn About SIBS Place
