Visit and Experience

It is possible to visit several schools, Batwa families and settlements and the Rafiki Memorial Wildlife Conservation center when you are in Bwindi for purposes of trekking to be among the mountain gorillas in Bwindi Impenetrable National Forest Park [Uganda]. The local rangers and porters will make your trek in the forest enjoyable and amazing!

Children, families, teachers and schools exist in the most remote villages everywhere in our world. We encourage visitors to spend time with the children….they are our future!

Natural Habitat Adventures

US Travel companies such as Natural Habitat Adventures [Boulder, Colorado] support local children with ongoing conservation and sustainability initiatives!

Nat Hab offers amazing treks to be among the mountain gorillas of Bwindi and can also introduce you to the local people.

Natural Habitat also offers the most unique small group adventure experiences throughout the world. When you travel with Nat Hab you support our natural environment, sustainability, endangered wildlife and most of all local children and families.
