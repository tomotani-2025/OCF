Get Involved

We encourage you to become involved with our projects by giving of your time and talents.

The Omotani Caring Foundation welcomes caring individuals who want to make a difference in the lives of children and families around the world.

Important: We do NOT actively solicit donations. We personally fund over 95% of the OCF projects and activities. The trustees independently and personally pay for all administrative, accounting and legal fees.
